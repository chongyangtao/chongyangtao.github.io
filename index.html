Chongyang Tao

Hi, I am Chongyang Tao, named after being born on Chung-Yeung Festival. Currently, I am a pre-tenure associate professor in the School of Computer Science and Engineering, Beihang University. Previously, I worked as a senior and postdoctoral scientist at Microsoft, respectively. I obtained the Ph.D. degree from Peking University in 2020, and the B. Eng degree from Sichuan University in 2015.
My research interests primarily lie in natural language processing and information retrieval, especially in deep learning models with their applications in dialogue systems, text generation, language understanding, and information retrieval.
