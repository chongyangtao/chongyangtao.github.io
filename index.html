Biography

Hi, I am Chongyang Tao, named after being born on Chung-Yeung Festival. Currently, I am a pre-tenure associate professor in the School of Computer Science and Engineering, Beihang University. Previously, I served as a postdoctoral and senior scientist at Microsoft, contributing to projects such as Microsoft Xiaoice, ChatwithBING, Bing Retrieval/Generative Model, and WizardLM. I obtained the Ph.D. degree from Peking University in 2020, and the B. Eng degree from Sichuan University in 2015.

My research lie in natural language processing and information retrieval, especially in deep learning models with their applications in dialogue systems, data intelligence, effective and generalizable IR. I have published over 70 papers in prestigious conferences and journals, including ACL, EMNLP, ICLR, NeurIPS, SIGIR, KDD and TOIS.
